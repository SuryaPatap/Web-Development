About Our University

Founded in 1985, Techville University has grown to become one of the leading institutions for technology and science education in the region. Our mission is to provide high-quality education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

With over 10,000 students and 500 faculty members, we offer a vibrant academic community with state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge research opportunities.

Our graduates are highly sought after by top employers worldwide, with a 95% employment rate within six months of graduation.

Academic Programs

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

    4-year program focusing on software development, algorithms, and computing theory.

  • Bachelor of Engineering

    Specializations in Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, and Computer Engineering.

  • Bachelor of Business Administration

    Comprehensive business education with concentrations in Finance, Marketing, and Management.

  • Master of Science in Computer Science

    Advanced study in computing with research opportunities and specializations.

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

    Professional degree focusing on business leadership and strategic management.

  • Master of Engineering

    Specialized technical education for engineering professionals.

  • PhD in Computer Science

    Research-intensive program preparing students for academic and industry research careers.

  • PhD in Engineering

    Advanced research in various engineering disciplines with faculty mentorship.

  • PhD in Business

    For those pursuing academic careers in business research and education.
